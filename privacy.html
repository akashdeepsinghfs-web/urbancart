Privacy Policy

Your trust matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

📋 1. Information We Collect

Urban Mart collects only the information necessary to provide you with a secure and seamless shopping experience. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, shipping address, phone number (only when you create an account, place an order, or contact us).
  • Payment Information: We do NOT store full credit card details. All payments are processed through PCI-DSS compliant gateways (Stripe/PayPal).
  • Browsing Data: Cookies and usage data to improve site functionality (no personal tracking for ads).
  • Order History: Details of purchases to assist with returns and customer support.

We never collect sensitive data without your explicit consent.

🔐 2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us deliver ethical and efficient service:

  • ✅ Process and fulfill your orders (including shipping confirmations).
  • ✅ Communicate important updates about your order or account.
  • ✅ Improve our website, product recommendations, and customer support.
  • ✅ Send promotional emails only if you opt-in — unsubscribe anytime.
  • ✅ Prevent fraud and enhance security.

Google Ads Compliance: Urban Mart does not misuse customer data for deceptive advertising. All marketing follows Google's strict policies.

🍪 3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies to enhance functionality:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for cart and checkout operations.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymous traffic analysis (via Google Analytics anonymized IP).
  • Preference Cookies: Remember language and region settings.

You can disable cookies in browser settings, but some features may be limited. We do not use cross-site tracking for ad retargeting without explicit consent.

🤝 4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell your personal information. Limited sharing occurs only for operational purposes:

  • Shipping Carriers: USPS, FedEx (to deliver your order).
  • Payment Processors: Stripe / PayPal (secure encrypted transactions).
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law or to protect our rights.

All third parties are contractually obligated to keep your data secure and use it only for specified services.

🛡️ 5. Data Security & Retention

We implement industry-standard safeguards:

  • 🔒 SSL/TLS encryption for all data transfers.
  • 🔒 Regular security audits and PCI compliance.
  • 🔒 Limited employee access to customer data.

We retain personal data only as long as necessary — typically 2 years after last interaction, unless legally required longer. You may request deletion anytime.

🌍 6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location (GDPR for EU, CCPA for California), you have the right to:

  • ✔️ Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • ✔️ Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • ✔️ Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • ✔️ Opt-out of marketing communications.
  • ✔️ Data portability (receive a copy of your data).

To exercise any right, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@urbanmart.com or use the form below.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

Urban Mart does not knowingly collect information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately for removal.

📢 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The "Effective Date" at the top will change. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Material changes will be notified via email or site banner.
